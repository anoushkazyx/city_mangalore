BEAHCES

When we imagine coastal lands, the first thing which comes to one's mind is coconut trees, golden sands, and pristine water and beaches in Mangalore are a utopia of this setting. From the ever clean Panambur beach to the picture-perfect Tannirbhavi beach, each one of them has something unique of its own.
